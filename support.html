Frequently asked questions

How do I scan a plant?

Open the app, tap the camera/scan button, and point it at the plant (or pick a photo from your library). You'll get an identification along with care tips.

How many scans do I get for free?

Free accounts include a set number of scans each week. You can see your remaining scans in the app. Upgrading to Premium removes the weekly limit.

What does Premium include, and how much is it?

Premium unlocks unlimited scans and the full set of care features. The current price is shown on the upgrade screen inside the app (Profile → Upgrade to Premium), localized to your region.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. To manage or cancel, open the iPhone Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → The Botanical Eye. Cancelling stops future renewals; access continues until the end of the current period.

I paid but Premium isn't active. How do I restore it?

In the app, open the paywall (Profile → Upgrade to Premium) and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe. If it still doesn't work, email us and we'll sort it out.

Is my data private?

Yes. See our Privacy Policy for exactly what we collect and how it's used.

A plant was identified incorrectly — what should I do?

AI identification is highly accurate but not perfect, especially in poor lighting or for very similar species. Try a clearer, well-lit photo of the leaves or flower. If results still seem off, let us know.
