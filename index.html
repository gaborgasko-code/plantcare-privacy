🌿 The Botanical Eye — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

PlantCare AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Photos: Images you take within the app are sent to Google Gemini AI for plant identification. Photos are not stored on our servers.
  • Device language: Used to provide responses in your preferred language.
  • Purchase information: Managed by RevenueCat and your app store. We do not store payment details.
  • Scan count: Stored locally on your device only to manage your free scan limit.

3. Camera Permission

PlantCare AI requires camera access solely to take photos of plants for identification purposes. We do not record video or access your camera without your explicit action.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify plants and provide health diagnosis using AI
  • To provide care recommendations in your language
  • To manage your subscription status

5. Third Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI: Processes plant photos for identification. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo: App framework. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain your photos. Scan counts are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

PlantCare AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at gaborgasko@gmail.com for any privacy requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
